Scope & Definitions

Amrutbaa Ni Cutney is a heritage food product made in weekly, fresh batches. “Customer”, “you”, or “user” refers to any person registering for a batch or using our site.

Ordering & Registration

Registrations must be completed 1 day prior to the batch preparation cutoff (typically Sunday midnight). Slots are limited and confirmed on a first-come basis.

Payments

Payments are processed through secure gateways. By paying, you authorize processing by the gateway provider. Payment failures should be resolved with the bank/gateway.

Preparation & Availability

Each batch is prepared fresh. We may reschedule or cancel due to ingredient constraints, force majeure, or safety concerns; we will notify you and offer alternatives/refunds where applicable.

Delivery

Dispatch typically begins the day after preparation. Accurate addresses and availability are required. Undeliverable orders may incur re-delivery fees.

Cancellations, Refunds & Returns

As a perishable product, cancellations and refunds are honored per our Refund Policy and Return Policy. Requests before the preparation cutoff are generally eligible; after preparation, refunds/returns are limited to damage/quality issues.

Quality & Food Safety

Prepared using traditional methods with strict hygiene. Refrigerate upon receipt and consume within the recommended period. Contains chili, garlic, and spices.
