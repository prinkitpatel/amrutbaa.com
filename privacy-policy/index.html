Data We Collect

  • Contact: name, email, phone.
  • Delivery: address, city, state, pincode.
  • Order: quantity and batch timing.
  • Payment metadata via secure gateways (we do not store full card or UPI credentials).

How We Use It

  • Confirm registrations and communicate schedules.
  • Process payments and coordinate delivery.
  • Share essential data with delivery partners solely for fulfillment.

Legal Basis & Consent

By registering, you consent to data use for processing and delivery. We may rely on legitimate interest for operational communications.

Sharing

We share minimal data with payment gateways and delivery partners to complete your order. We do not sell your data.

Retention & Security

Data is retained only as long as needed for legal, accounting, and operations. We apply reasonable safeguards; no method is 100% secure.

Your Rights

  • Access, correction, deletion (subject to law).
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.

Contact

For privacy queries, contact us via your registration reference or homepage contact. We aim to respond within 3–5 business days.
