Refund Policy

Fresh batch preparation impacts eligibility for refunds. Here’s how it works.

Contents:
  • Eligibility
  • Damaged or Incorrect Delivery
  • Process & Timelines
  • Non-Refundable
  • Contact

Eligibility

  • Requests before preparation cutoff (usually Sunday midnight) are generally eligible for a full refund.
  • Requests after preparation are limited to damage, leakage, or quality concerns.
  • Perishable food once prepared and dispatched is not eligible for change-of-mind refunds.

Damaged or Incorrect Delivery

Report within 24 hours of delivery, with photos of the package and seal. We will inspect and offer a replacement or refund based on the finding.

Process & Timelines

  • Write to us using your registration confirmation email.
  • Approved refunds are initiated within 5–7 business days to the original payment method via the gateway.
  • Bank processing times may vary.

Non-Refundable

  • Incorrect address or recipient unavailability after multiple attempts.
  • Product mishandled post-delivery (e.g., not refrigerated as instructed).
  • Allergies not disclosed; ingredients are listed on the site.

Contact

Use the contact in your registration confirmation or the homepage to raise a request. Include your order reference, photos (if any), and a brief description.
