Privacy Policy

We collect only essential information required to run our weekly batch registration, prepare your order, and deliver it safely.

Data We Collect

  • Contact: name, email, phone.
  • Delivery: address, city, state, pincode.
  • Order details: quantity and batch timing.
  • Payment metadata via secure gateways (we do not store full card or UPI credentials).

How We Use It

  • Confirm registrations and communicate batch schedules.
  • Process payments and coordinate delivery.
  • Share essential data with delivery partners solely for fulfillment.

Legal Basis & Consent

By registering, you consent to the use of your data for order processing and delivery. Where applicable, we rely on legitimate interest to ensure smooth operations of the weekly batch model.

Sharing

We share minimal data with payment gateways and delivery partners to complete your order. We do not sell or lease your personal information to third parties for marketing.

Retention & Security

Data is retained only as long as needed for legal, accounting, and operational purposes. We implement reasonable safeguards; however, no method is 100% secure. Please avoid sharing sensitive payment details beyond the payment gateway interface.

Your Rights

  • Access, correction, or deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements).
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications.
  • Withdraw consent where processing relies on it.

Contact

For privacy queries, reach us via your registration email reference or the homepage contact. We aim to respond within 3–5 business days.
