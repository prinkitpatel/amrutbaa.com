Return Policy

Freshly prepared, perishable goods have limited return scenarios to ensure safety and quality.

Contents:
  • Not Eligible
  • Eligible
  • How to Report
  • Contact

Not Eligible

  • Change of mind after preparation/dispatch.
  • Taste preference; recipe is traditional and spicy.
  • Improper storage post-delivery (e.g., not refrigerated).

Eligible

  • Damaged packaging or tampered seal at delivery.
  • Incorrect item or quantity received.

How to Report

  • Notify us within 24 hours of delivery with photos.
  • We will review and offer a replacement or refund as appropriate.

Contact

Use your registration confirmation email or homepage contact to initiate a return-related query. Include your order reference and photos.
