Policy

Refund Policy

Because we prepare fresh, scheduled batches, refund eligibility depends on timing and the nature of the issue.

Eligibility

  • Requests before preparation cutoff (usually Sunday midnight) are generally eligible for a full refund.
  • Requests after preparation are limited to issues of damage, leakage, or quality concerns.
  • Perishable food once prepared and dispatched is not eligible for change-of-mind refunds.

Damaged or Incorrect Delivery

Please report within 24 hours of delivery, with photos of the package and seal. We will inspect and offer replacement or refund based on the finding.

Process & Timelines

  • Write to us using your registration confirmation email.
  • Approved refunds are initiated within 5–7 business days to the original payment method via the gateway (e.g., PhonePe).
  • Bank processing times may vary.

Non-Refundable

  • Incorrect address or recipient unavailability after multiple attempts.
  • Product mishandled post-delivery (e.g., not refrigerated as instructed).
  • Allergies not disclosed; ingredients are listed on the site.

Contact

Use the contact in your registration confirmation or the homepage to raise a request. Please include your order reference, photos (if any), and a brief description.
