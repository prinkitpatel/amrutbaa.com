Policy

Shipping Policy

We ship fresh jars soon after a weekly batch is prepared. Timelines depend on your location and courier availability.

Dispatch

  • Preparation is completed on the scheduled batch day (typically Sunday).
  • Dispatch begins next day after preparation, subject to courier operations.

Delivery Timeframes

  • Metro/nearby: 1–3 business days after dispatch.
  • Other locations: 3–7 business days after dispatch.
  • Remote areas may take longer based on serviceability.

Charges

Shipping fees, if applicable, are shown at checkout/registration. Re-delivery due to incorrect address or recipient unavailability may incur additional charges.

Address Accuracy

Please provide accurate address and landmark details. Failed deliveries due to incorrect addresses may not qualify for refunds per our Refund Policy.

Tracking

We share dispatch updates via email/SMS when available. Courier tracking links may be provided where supported.

Contact

For shipping queries, reply to your registration confirmation or use the homepage contact. Include your order reference for faster assistance.
