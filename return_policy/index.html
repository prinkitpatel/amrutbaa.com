Return Policy

Our chutney is freshly prepared and perishable. Returns are limited to specific scenarios to ensure safety and quality.

Not Eligible

  • Change of mind after preparation/dispatch.
  • Taste preference; recipe is traditional and spicy by nature.
  • Improper storage post-delivery (e.g., not refrigerated).

Eligible

  • Damaged packaging or tampered seal at delivery.
  • Incorrect item or quantity received.

How to Report

  • Notify us within 24 hours of delivery with photos.
  • We will review and offer a replacement or refund as appropriate.

Contact

Use your registration confirmation email or the homepage contact to initiate a return-related query. Include order reference and photos.
