Shipping Policy

Fresh jars are shipped shortly after batch preparation. Timelines depend on your location and courier serviceability.

Contents:

  • Delivery Timeframes
  • Charges
  • Address Accuracy

Dispatch

  • Batch is prepared on the scheduled day (typically Sunday).
  • Dispatch begins the next day after preparation, subject to courier operations.

Delivery Timeframes

  • Metro/nearby: 1–3 business days after dispatch.
  • Other locations: 3–7 business days after dispatch.
  • Remote areas may take longer based on serviceability.

Charges

Shipping fees, if applicable, are shown at registration. Re-delivery due to incorrect address or unavailability may incur additional charges.

Address Accuracy

Provide accurate address and landmark details. Failed deliveries due to incorrect addresses may not qualify for refunds per our Refund Policy.

Tracking

We share dispatch updates via email/SMS when available. Courier tracking links may be provided where supported.

Contact

For shipping queries, reply to your registration confirmation or use the homepage contact. Include your order reference.
